Kyla is a student at St. Olaf College studying biology on the pre-dental track. She is doing research this summer at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. She is interested in immune responses and oral wound healing to better understand recovery processes for her future patients. In the future, she would like to provide restorative care to patients who cannot afford treatment and to reduce barriers to oral health care in rural areas. Outside of academics, she is a mentor for Project Friendship and volunteers with APO. In her free time, she enjoys reading, going on long walks, and spending time with family and friends.
